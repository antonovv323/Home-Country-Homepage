Visit Bulgaria

Immerse yourself in the culture of Bulgaria, a blend of history, sports, and natural beauty.

Top three activities to do at Bulgaria

Attend a Traditional Folklore Performance

Experience Bulgaria’s rich folklore heritage with a live performance showcasing traditional music and dances in cities like Sofia or Plovdiv.

Go Hiking in the Rila Mountains

Enjoy a scenic hike through the Rila Mountains, home to stunning views, alpine lakes, and the famous Seven Rila Lakes.

Experience Skiing in Bansko

Hit the slopes in Bulgaria’s top ski resort, Bansko, where you can enjoy skiing and snowboarding in a picturesque mountain setting.
